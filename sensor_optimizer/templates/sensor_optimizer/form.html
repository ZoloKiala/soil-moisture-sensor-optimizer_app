🌱 Soil Moisture Sensor Optimization

Sentinel-1 + ExtraTrees — compare candidate grid sizes and select the most efficient sensor layout.

Methodology. The app first estimates soil moisture on a 10 m surface across the uploaded field polygon. It then evaluates candidate sensor grids inside that polygon by aggregating each candidate grid cell as a polygon-based soil-moisture unit, computes the coefficient of variation (CV) across those grid-cell values, and selects the fewest sensors with CV below 10% and at least 2 sensors; if none meet that threshold, it falls back to the minimum-CV layout.
